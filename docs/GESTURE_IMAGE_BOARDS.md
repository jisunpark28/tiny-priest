# Tiny-Church Gesture Image Boards

This folder contains generated image boards used to verify character rendering and gesture implementation.

## Main Boards

- `character-parts-board.png`  
  Front/back source character images for priest and nun.

- `gesture-breakdown-priest.png`  
  Priest gesture sequence screenshots (Idle, Pray, Point, Hold, Lift, Our Father, Sign of Cross).

- `gesture-breakdown-nun.png`  
  Nun gesture sequence screenshots (Idle, Pray, Point, Hold, Lift, Our Father, Sign of Cross).

- `priest-gesture-bodyparts-board.png`  
  Priest-focused body-part breakdown board with overlay boxes for:
  - Head
  - Body
  - Left Arm
  - Right Arm
  - Hands
